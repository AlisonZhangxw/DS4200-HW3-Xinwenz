My Hobby: Sports

Sports are an essential part of my life. No matter how busy I get with my studies, I always try to make time for exercise because it keeps me clear-minded, relaxed, and confident. For me, sports are not just about staying fit â€” they are also a way to connect with myself. Every moment of sweat and effort reminds me of the value of persistence.

My three favorite activities are swimming, skiing, and Pilates. Swimming helps me completely relax and let my thoughts drift with the water. Skiing gives me a sense of speed and freedom; when I rush down the slopes, I forget all my worries. Pilates, on the other hand, teaches me how to control my body, stay balanced, and take care of myself in a gentle yet effective way.

Sometimes I can be lazy and just stay at home doing nothing, but exercising always brings back my energy and motivation. In the future, I hope to keep this passion alive, explore more types of sports, and continue to live an active, passionate life while becoming stronger inside and out.

My Favorite Sports:

  • Swimming: Relaxing both body and mind in the water
  • Snowboard: Feeling the thrill of speed and freedom in nature
  • Pilates: Building core strength and maintaining body balance
  • Cycling: Exploring the city and nature in the wind
  • Running: The simplest and most effective way to release stress
